Kesharpura Population - Sabarkantha, Gujarat

Kesharpura is a large village located in Idar Taluka of Sabarkantha district, Gujarat with total 724 families residing. The Kesharpura village has population of 4130 of which 2069 are males while 2061 are females as per Population Census 2011. In Kesharpura village population of children with age 0-6 is 585 which makes up 14.16 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Kesharpura village is 996 which is higher than Gujarat state average of 919. Child Sex Ratio for the Kesharpura as per census is 918, higher than Gujarat average of 890. Kesharpura village has higher literacy rate compared to Gujarat. In 2011, literacy rate of Kesharpura village was 89.45 % compared to 78.03 % of Gujarat. In Kesharpura Male literacy stands at 95.24 % while female literacy rate was 83.72 %. As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Kesharpura village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.
